MYSELF AND MY FAMILY

1.¿Cómo te llamas?

2.¿Cuál es tu apellido?

3.¿Cuántos años tienes?

4.¿Dónde vives?

5.¿Cuál es tu dirección?

6.¿Cuánto tiempo hace que vives allá?

7.¿Dime algo de tu vecindad/pueblo?

8.¿Dónde prefieres vivir-en el campo o en la ciudad?

9.¿Cuántas personas hay en tu familia/viven en tu casa?¿Quiénes son?

10.¿Cuántos hermanos(mayores/menores) tienes?

11.¿Cuántos años tiene el mayor/el menor?

12.¿Quién es la persona más interesante/inteligente/simpatica de tu familia?

13.¿Quién es la persona más generosa de tu familia y por que la describes así?

14.¿Cómo eres?

15.¿Crees que son/deben ser estrictos tus padres?

16.¿Qué piensas de tu familia?

17.¿Qué le gusta hacer tu papa/mama en su tiempo libre?

18.¿Cuáles son los programas de televisión preferidos de tu familia?

19.¿Cuáles vacaciones prefiere tu familia: las vacaciones de Navidad o las de verano?¿Por qué?

20.¿Quién prepara las comidas para tu familia?

21.¿Describe la comida favorita de tu familia?

22.¿Cómo es una comida con toda la familia?

24.¿Describe un fin de semana típico?

25.¿Cómo pasa tu familia los fines de semana?

27.¿Cómo celebra tu familia la Navidad?

29.¿En que año naciste?

30.¿Dónde naciste?

10. ¿Tienen ustedes un baile de fin de curso?

11. ¿Dónde tendrá lugar?

15. ¿Crees que algún día tu serás buen/a profesor/a? ¿Por qué?

16. ¿Cuáles son las características de un buen estudiante?

RESPONSES TO SITUATIONS

1.Your after school lessons teacher asks you what Spanish grammar topic you don't understand.

(a). What do you say? (b) What does the teacher suggest?

2.Your friends have organized a graduation party for after exams but you don't have a ride.

(a). What do you ask one friend? (b) What does your friend offer to do?

3.Your girlfriend asks you what it is about her that annoys you most.

(a). What do you respond? (b) What do you suggest?

4. Your Spanish teacher is working his heart out and your classmate isn't paying attention.

(a). What do you say to the classmate? (b) What does your classmate respond?

5.Your receive a fb invite to a party however your parents banned you for something you did.

(a). What does your father say to you? (b) What does he explain?

6.You failed your coursework for your Math class because of a difficulty you had.

(a). What excuse do you give to your teacher? (b) What request do you make?

7. Your church is visiting a Spanish speaking country and you are asked to make a small presentation about T&T.

(a). How do you introduce your country? (b) What do you offer to the members there?

8. You hope to visit Costa Rica with your school next year but your parents think otherwise.

(a). What do you tell your parents about the trip? (b) What do you promise to do?

9.Your teacher asks what is your favourite Spanish textbook.

(a). What do you respond? (b) What reason do you give?

10. Your mother calls you at home because she has gone shopping and realizes she forgot her purse.

(a). What does she ask you to do? (b) What do you suggest?

11. Your school cleaner is sweeping the room during class time.

(a). What does your teacher explain to the cleaner? (b) What does your teacher suggest?

12.Your teacher complains to your parents that you have been rude to him/her.

(a). What do you explain to your parents? (b) What do you promise to do?
